Local story & mission

Stilo Supply opened its doors in the Bixby Knolls neighbourhood to give Long Beach better weed and a place to connect. Instead of feeling like another corporate chain, our shop has a familiar, community‑driven vibe. We partner directly with top cultivators—no middlemen or inflated markups—so you get fresh flower, clean concentrates and premium edibles at fair prices. We stock products we would use ourselves and welcome everyone from first‑timers to seasoned connoisseurs.
