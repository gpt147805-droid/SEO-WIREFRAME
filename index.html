Frequently asked questions

Do you deliver?

Yes—place an order online to get cannabis delivered the same day throughout Long Beach and surrounding areas.

Are out‑of‑state visitors welcome?

Absolutely! Bring a valid government‑issued ID proving you’re 21 or older, or a doctor’s recommendation if you’re a medical patient under 21.

What payment methods are accepted for weed purchases?

We accept cash, debit and credit cards for weed purchases; there’s also an ATM on site for your convenience.

What are the first‑time customer deals?

Spin our prize wheel for up to 30 % off your first weed purchase, get 25 % off your second visit and 20 % off your third.

How does the rewards programme work?

You’ll earn 1 point per dollar spent on weed. Redeem points for birthday deals, referral bonuses and rotating perks—plus, check back each Monday for fresh weekly weed specials.

How far is Stilo Supply from Cal State Long Beach?

We’re only about ten minutes away via the 405 freeway. Take the Long Beach Blvd exit and you’ll find us in the Bixby Knolls neighbourhood.

Do you deliver to Signal Hill?

Yes! We offer same‑day weed delivery to Signal Hill and most of Long Beach. Simply place your order online and we’ll bring it right to your door.
